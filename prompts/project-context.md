Project: ReturnOS

ReturnOS is an MVP SaaS for customer loyalty and reactivation for small food businesses in LATAM, especially bakeries and restaurants.

Main value:
Help businesses make customers come back using:

- QR customer registration
- visit tracking
- rewards
- inactive customer detection
- reactivation campaigns

Tech stack:

- Next.js App Router
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase
- Minimal MVP approach

Important rules:

- Keep implementation simple
- Avoid overengineering
- Prefer readability over abstraction
- Mobile-friendly where relevant
- Use reusable utility functions for business logic
- Focus on speed to market
